Index

Chord Dictionary

Index is a simple yet powerful rack extension for discovering and exploring chords. With two novel lookup methods and an intuitive chord visualization feature it is sure to be an invaluable tool for all aspiring songwriters and keyboardists. Index helps you build chord progressions, find that chord you hear in your head but can't seem to find on the keyboard, and enhances your ability to jam with other musicians. Wherever you're at on your musical journey, Index will expand your understanding of music and get you the results you want to hear.
