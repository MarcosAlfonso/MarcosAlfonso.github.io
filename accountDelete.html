Personal Information and Account Deletion Requests for Omni and other Static Cling Apps and Services

To request to review, update, or delete your personal information, please email us at privacy@staticcling.io with your Player ID and specifics of your request.

We will consider and act upon any request in accordance with applicable data protection laws. Types of data that can be requested/deleted are Account Data and Diagnostic Data.

Your Player ID can be found on the Settings screen, accessed by clicking the Gear icon on the Main Menu or from the "Settings" button of the Pause Screen.

Your Player ID will be near the bottom the screen, where it says ID: [Player ID]. It can be copied to your device's clipboard by clicking the Copy icon to the right of the Player ID.
